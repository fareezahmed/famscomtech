Our Dry Cleaning Process

A comprehensive 5-step process that ensures your garments receive the best care possible

1

Inspection & Tagging

Each garment is carefully inspected for stains, damage, and fabric type. Items are tagged with unique identifiers for tracking.

2

Pre-Treatment

Stains are identified and treated with specialized solutions before the main cleaning process begins.

3

Eco-Friendly Cleaning

Garments are cleaned using our certified eco-friendly solvents that are gentle on fabrics and the environment.

4

Quality Inspection

Every item undergoes a thorough quality check to ensure stains are removed and the garment is properly cleaned.

5

Professional Finishing

Final pressing and finishing to restore the garment to its original appearance and ensure a crisp, professional look.
